## Health Insurance Requirements - Monthly Payment Policy

### Question: Are health insurance policies with monthly payments considered acceptable?

**Answer:** No, they are not acceptable.

**Official Statement:** "Eligible policies are those with premiums covering a full year, paid in advance. A receipt for this payment may be requested."

---

## Key Compliance Points

1. **Monthly Payments BANNED:** Health insurance policies with monthly payment plans are explicitly NOT acceptable
2. **Annual Payment Required:** Premiums must cover a full year
3. **Payment in Advance:** The full annual premium must be paid upfront
4. **Receipt Required:** Applicants must be able to provide proof of full-year advance payment

---

## Implication for Insurance Products

This requirement **automatically disqualifies**:
- Monthly subscription insurance (e.g., SafetyWing, World Nomads with monthly billing)
- Quarterly payment plans
- Any payment cadence shorter than annual

**Only acceptable:**
- Annual policies with full premium paid in advance
- Multi-year policies with advance payment
