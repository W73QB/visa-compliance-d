# Thailand Digital Nomad Visa (DTV) Requirements

**Source:** Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Thailand - Thai E-Visa Official Website  
**URL:** https://www.thaievisa.go.th/visa/dtv-visa  
**Retrieved:** 2026-01-12  

## Visa Điểm đến Thái Lan (DTV)
Các hoạt động Workcation và liên quan đến sức mạnh mềm của Thái Lan

---

## Category 1: Workcation (digital nomad/remote worker/foreign talent/freelancer)

### Required Documents
*(Please check the relevant embassy/consulate website for specific required documents)*

1. Personal information page in passport or travel document
2. Photo taken within the past six months
3. Document confirming current location
4. Financial proof: amount not less than 500,000 THB, For example: bank statement for the past 3 months or official confirmation letter
5. Employment contract or labor certificate in their country or professional portfolio proving digital nomad, remote worker, foreign talent or freelancer status

**Number of entries:** Multiple  
**Visa validity:** 5 Years  
**Accepted passport types:** Ordinary passport, Official passport

---

## Category 2: Activities related to Thailand's soft power (e.g. Muaythai, Thai culinary training, medical treatment)

### Required Documents
*(Please check the relevant embassy/consulate website for specific required documents)*

1. Personal information page in passport or travel document
2. Photo taken within the past six months
3. Document confirming current location
4. Financial proof: amount not less than 500,000 THB, For example: bank statement for the past 3 months or official confirmation letter
5. Certificate of participation in activities or invitation letter from hospital/medical center

**Number of entries:** Multiple  
**Visa validity:** 5 Years  
**Accepted passport types:** Ordinary passport, Official passport

---

## Category 3: Spouse and children under 20 years of DTV visa holder

### Required Documents
*(Please check the relevant embassy/consulate website for specific required documents)*

1. Personal information page in passport or travel document
2. Photo taken within the past six months
3. Document confirming current location
4. Financial proof: amount not less than 500,000 THB, For example: bank statement for the past 3 months or official confirmation letter
5. DTV visa of the DTV holder
6. Proof of relationship with DTV visa holder, for example: copy of marriage certificate / birth certificate / child adoption certificate

**Number of entries:** Multiple  
**Visa validity:** 5 Years  
**Accepted passport types:** Ordinary passport, Official passport

---

## Key Finding: Insurance Not Required

**IMPORTANT:** Nowhere in the official requirements list does the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Thailand mention insurance, health insurance, medical insurance, or travel insurance as a requirement for the Digital Nomad Visa (DTV).

The complete list of required documents has been captured above, and insurance is NOT among them.
