## Article 9: Medical Insurance Requirements (Artículo 9)

### Coverage Duration
**Spanish:** "La vigencia de la póliza deberá cubrir totalmente la permanencia legal autorizada"

**English Translation:** The policy must cover the entire authorized legal stay period.

### Minimum Coverage Amount
The insurance must guarantee "gastos médicos en casos de enfermedades en Costa Rica por al menos US $50 000,00"

**English Translation:** Medical expenses for illnesses in Costa Rica for at least US$50,000.00

### Authorized Providers
Insurance can be obtained through:
- Entities authorized by Costa Rica's insurance superintendent (Superintendencia de Seguros)
- International policies meeting the specified coverage requirements

### Family Coverage
Each family member requires their own separate policy meeting the same duration and coverage amount standards.

---

## Key Compliance Points

1. **Mandatory Insurance:** Health insurance is required for all Digital Nomad visa applicants
2. **Full Period Coverage:** The policy must cover the ENTIRE authorized stay - monthly/cancellable subscriptions may not meet this requirement
3. **Minimum Amount:** At least $50,000 USD coverage for medical expenses in Costa Rica
4. **Individual Policies:** Each family member needs a separate policy
