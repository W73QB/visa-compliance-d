## What the authority requires

Verified subset as of 2026-01-16. Each item links to an official authority page or program site.

- Bahamas (BEATS): proof of medical insurance for applicant and dependents. Source: https://www.bahamasbeats.com/faq
- Antigua and Barbuda (Nomad Digital Residence): proof of medical insurance and travel insurance. Source: https://nomad.gov.ag/ui/faq.php
- Curacao (@HOME in Curacao): international travel insurance required; medical insurance is a prerequisite. Source: https://athomeincuracao.com/faq/
- Belize (Work Where You Vacation): travel insurance minimum 50,000 USD. Source: https://www.travelbelize.org/work-where-you-vacation/
